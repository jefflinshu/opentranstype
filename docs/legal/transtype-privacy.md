# Transtype Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-03

## Summary

Transtype is a local macOS writing and translation utility. It reads text from supported macOS text inputs through Apple's Accessibility APIs and translates text with Apple's on-device Translation framework. Transtype does not require account login, does not run a cloud backend for translation, and does not sell or share personal data for advertising.

## Information We Process

Transtype may process the following information on your device:

- text you type into supported text fields
- text you manually select for translation
- your selected target language
- local translation history stored on your Mac
- Accessibility permission status
- locally available Apple translation language packs

## How We Use Information

We use this information only to:

- detect and read text from the active macOS text field when permission is granted
- translate text on device using Apple's Translation framework
- replace the original text when you choose to apply the translated result
- remember basic app preferences and local translation history
- help you check whether required on-device language packs are available

## On-Device Processing

Translation is designed around Apple's on-device Translation framework and installed Apple language packs. Based on the current codebase, Transtype does not send translation content to a Transtype-operated cloud service for processing.

## Permissions

Transtype requests or relies on the following system capabilities:

- Accessibility access: required to inspect supported text inputs and help apply translated text back into the active app

If you do not grant Accessibility access, core translation overlay behavior will not work.

## Data Storage

Translation history and app preferences are stored locally on your device. Diagnostic logs may also be written locally for troubleshooting. We do not maintain a remote user account system for this app.

## Data Sharing

Transtype does not sell personal information and does not share typed content with third-party advertisers. Content may be handled by Apple system frameworks on your device as part of translation and language pack management.

## Security

We aim to minimize data movement by keeping translation and history handling local to your Mac whenever possible. No method of storage or processing is absolutely secure, but the app is designed to avoid unnecessary network transmission of user text.

## Children's Privacy

Transtype is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through a remote service.

## Your Choices

You can:

- deny or revoke Accessibility access in macOS System Settings
- clear local translation history inside the app
- remove the app and its local data from your device

## Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or platform updates. The latest version should be published at the app's privacy policy URL.
